Work Day Scheduler

A simple calendar app for scheduling your work day.

Input fields for past hours will display gray. The current hour will display red. The future hours, green.

Go ahead, type in your schedule and click the save button! It will all be there, even if you refresh! Day over? Wipe the slate clean with the Clear All button below.
